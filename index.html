PREMIER CRYPTO EXCHANGE

Trade your cryptos on a fully licenced exchange, compliant with all EU regulations.

HIGH FREQUENCY TRADING

The alpha build of our matching engine can process about 4 million trades per second, per trading pair. You can expect this number to grow in the future, as we keep accommodating your HFT needs.

QUALITY LISTINGS

EUCX does not intend to list every possible coin. We want you to feel safe by knowing that the coins or tokens available have been scrutinized by us and the community before being made available for trading on EUCX.

REGULATED

ENTERPRISE-GRADE SECURITY

We have extensive experience in IT-environments that undergo the most severe scrutiny of the information systems through a secure software development process, code reviews, penetration testing, organizational measures, etc. Proper information security is therefore known territory, allowing us to properly integrate security measures into the organization from the get-go, instead of retrofitting it at a later stage. For this reason we have a bug bounty program as of our Vulnerability Disclosure Policy, where ethical hackers and security experts can receive recognition and financial compensation for reporting bugs and possible exploits. All this is done so that you, our customer, can be confident that your funds are as safe as humanly possible.

SITE RELIABILITY

Remembers those times when you had to buy or sell that one special coin or token, and the exchange was down because everyone else thought the same? We do too. A platform such as a cryptocurrency exchange needs to be up and running at all time; no excuses. As with security, site reliability engineering is a feature that needs to be integrated into the IT-systems and organization, rather than be retrofitted. We have designed your trading platform to be scalable and therefore resilient against sudden peaks and constant volume in market activity.

FINACIAL PRODUCTS

To facilitate the growing demand for cryptocurrency-based financial products, we set out to create numerous financial products in 2019.Several of these products can be traded with leverage. Currently planned are:

  • Futures Contracts
  • ETFs
  • Index Funds

The close discourse that we have with the regulatory bodies in the Netherlands allow us to quickly and effectively create professional financial products based on cryptocurrencies, without users having to stress over regulatory issues.
